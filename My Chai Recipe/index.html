How to make MASALA Tea

Masala chai also known simply as chai, is an Indian beverage popular throughout South Asia. Masala Chai or Masala Tea is an Indian spiced tea made with whole spices, water, black tea and milk. At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Cardmom powder
  • Clove powder
  • Ginger 1 inch
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar

preparation

  1. Take a saucepan. Pour 2 glasses of water and 1 glass of Milk
  2. Put crushed Ginger to the saucepan
  3. Put 1/2 teaspoon of cardmom powder
  4. Put 1/4 teaspoon of clove powder
  5. Add 2 and 1/2 spoons of sugar
  6. Add 1 teaspoon full of tea powder
  7. Boil everything for 10 minnutes in slow simmer mode
  8. Sieve the Masala tea to 2 cups
  9. Serve Masala Tea along with Biscuits or Cookies
  10. Enjoy!!!
